# Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 6, 2026

YouTube Subscriptions Fixer is a browser extension that changes the way the YouTube Subscriptions page is displayed in your browser.

## Data Collection

The extension does not collect, sell, rent, or transmit personal information.

The extension stores your feature preferences, such as whether to hide Shorts or show four videos per row, using Chrome storage. If Chrome sync is enabled in your browser, Chrome may sync those settings through your Google account according to your browser settings.

## YouTube Page Access

The extension runs on `https://www.youtube.com/*` so it can hide or style page elements on YouTube. It reads the YouTube page structure in your browser only to apply user-facing display changes. It does not send YouTube browsing activity, video history, subscriptions, account information, or page contents to the developer or to any external server.

## Third Parties

The extension does not use analytics, advertising, remote code, or third-party tracking services.

## Data Sharing

The developer does not receive or share user data from this extension.

## Contact

For privacy questions, use the support contact listed on the Chrome Web Store listing.
